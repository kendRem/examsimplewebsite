Provocans Ad Volandum!

St Xavier's college is private college founded in 1869 by the Jesuits with a vision of creating an academic community dedicated to the holistic education of future leaders who will demonstrate innovation in their professional competencies, integration in their personal lives and inclusion in their social contribution.

Our motto "Provocans Ad Volandum!" translates to “Challenging to Fly”,which reinforces our Key Values of Innovation, Integration, Inclusion
